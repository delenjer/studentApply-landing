Global platform for applying to universities all over the world

About the Project

This platform is a revolutionary web-based platform which allows students and student-recruiters to apply to multiple universities, colleges, and high schools across numerous countries with just one, simple application.

Main Tasks

For prospective students - to make the process of making applications to university as simple and comfortable as possible. For partner agents - to create a convenient tool for working with students and universities.

Main Users

This platform gives separate access for such users

Students

Prospective students who want to study in foreign universities.

Partners

Institutions that are interested in attracting the best students.

Agents

Student recruiters who help manage the admission process.

For Students:

Quickly find a suitable education institution

Convenient search for universities; viewing admission requirements, tuition fees, admission campaign deadlines; viewing video presentations of universities, available faculties and study campuses.

Fill profile and create Application

The ability to fill out an application form for admission and send it for consideration; the opportunity to use the advice of an admission specialist; assistance in preparing a CV and passing an interview at the university.

Choose personal plan

Ability to choose personal plans allows students to submit the required number of applications to universities.

For Agents and Partners:

Create Students and Applications

Filling out student information and creating applications to the desired university.

Training materials and comision rate

Access to a personalized commission rate for each university and platform training materials.

Applications tracking

Each agent can track the application status, update documents, send and receive notifications.

Training materials and comision rate

Access to a personalized commission rate for each university and platform training materials.
